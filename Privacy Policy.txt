

Third-Party Advertising Services
​To keep our app free to use, we partner with third-party advertising networks, specifically Unity Ads and AppLovin. These providers may automatically collect and process certain information when you use our app to serve interest-based advertisements and provide analytics.
​Information Collected by Third Parties:
These services may collect technical data such as your device’s advertising identifier (e.g., Google Advertising ID), IP address, device model, and information about your interactions with the app and advertisements.
​How This Data is Used:
This data is used to:
​Serve relevant, personalized advertisements.
​Measure the performance of our advertising campaigns.
​Prevent fraudulent activity.
​For More Information:
We encourage you to review the privacy policies of our partners to understand how they handle your data:
​Unity Ads Privacy Policy: https://unity.com/legal/game-player-and-app-user-privacy-policy?hl=en-US
​AppLovin Privacy Policy: https://www.applovin.com/en
​You can typically opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device’s privacy settings (e.g., "Reset Advertising ID" or "Opt-out of Ads Personalization" in your Android settings).
